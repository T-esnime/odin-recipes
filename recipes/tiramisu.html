Tiramisu

Who doesn't love TIRAMISU !

This mouthwatering italian dessert is surprisingly not hard to make. Just follow the steps below and enjoy the sweetest of all desserts.

Classic tiramisu, with its irresistible coffee flavor and lightly sweetened mascarpone, will never go out of style. This tiramisu recipe is a no-bake dessert that's sure to impress even the pickiest of eaters.

Ingredients :

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Egg Yolks
  • Cream
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Ladyfingers
  • Cocoa Powder

Steps :

  1. Make the filling
  2. Make the whipped cream
  3. Soak the Ladyfingers
  4. Assemble the tiramisu
  5. Put the tiramisu in the fridge
  6. Enjoy your dessert
