Cinnamon Rolls

Super yummy CINNAMON ROLLS

An easy to make dessert. Just follow the steps below and enjoy the sweetest of all desserts.

Ingredients :

  • Dough
  • Filling
  • Cream cheese frosting

Steps :

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the filling
  3. Roll dough around the filling
  4. Bake the rolls
  5. Top the rolls with the frosting
  6. Enjoy your dessert
